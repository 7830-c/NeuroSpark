NeuroSpark introduces a groundbreaking approach to disease detection in neurological healthcare by harnessing Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs). In today's healthcare landscape, accurate detection of neurological disorders from brain MRI images is pivotal for effective patient care. NeuroSpark addresses this challenge by leveraging deep learning techniques to predict multiple major brain diseases concurrently from MRI images. The project encompasses the development and evaluation of a custom CNN model, alongside testing various pre-trained models. Among these models, Xception emerges as the top performer, surpassing both the custom model and other pre-trained alternatives.

The success of NeuroSpark is further augmented by the integration of advanced techniques such as dropout layers and early stopping. These techniques enhance the model's generalization performance, ensuring robustness and reliability in disease detection. NeuroSpark signifies a significant advancement in AI-driven disease detection within neurological healthcare, with the potential to greatly improve patient outcomes and healthcare delivery. By accurately predicting multiple major brain diseases from MRI images, NeuroSpark promises enhanced diagnostic accuracy and efficiency, ultimately paving the way for improved patient care and healthcare delivery in neurological healthcare.
